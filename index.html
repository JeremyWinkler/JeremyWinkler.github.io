Lamport's Legion

This page will be for our group (consisting of the team members listed below) to post our assignment submissions and useful links. The purpose of this project will be to understand the software architecture chosen to develop the IntelliJ IDEA application. We will be looking at the architecture from both a conceptual and concrete perspective, by both investigating how the architecture was originally designed, as well as reverse-engineering the source code to elicit the concrete architecture.
